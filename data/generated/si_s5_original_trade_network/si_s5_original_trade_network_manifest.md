# Supplementary Figure S5 original trade network

Generated from DGCIS interstate cereal trade inputs averaged over 2016-2018.
Panel a combines jowar, bajra, maize, and ragi in kilocalories.
Panel b combines rice and wheat in kilocalories.
Panel a displayed states: west bengal, andhra pradesh, assam, bihar, chhattisgarh, gujarat, haryana, karnataka, kerala, madhya pradesh, maharashtra, odisha, punjab, rajasthan, tamil nadu, telangana, uttar pradesh, uttarakhand.
Panel b displayed states: west bengal, andhra pradesh, assam, bihar, chhattisgarh, delhi, gujarat, haryana, jharkhand, jammu and kashmir, karnataka, kerala, madhya pradesh, maharashtra, odisha, punjab, rajasthan, tamil nadu, telangana, uttar pradesh.
