# Revenue profit sensitivity audit

This SI-only figure reruns the primary endpoint solves under a realized-price
revenue benchmark. For each year scenario, official realized state-year unit prices
are used where matched state-level data exist. For state-crop combinations without matched
realized-price data, the original district MSP benchmark is scaled by the corresponding
all-India realized-price/MSP multiplier for that crop and year.

Panel a displays only the 2017-18 realized-price benchmark because this is the year
most closely aligned with the 2017 baseline optimization used in the manuscript.
For 2017-18, direct realized-price coverage is 94.06% of decision keys
and 99.98% of baseline cereal area.
Across realized-price scenarios, direct realized-price coverage spans 92.92-94.06% of decision keys
and 99.54-99.98% of baseline cereal area. Crop-year row coverage ranges from
27.8% (Ragi, 2016-17)
to 94.4% (Rice, 2014-15).
